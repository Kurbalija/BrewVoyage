Welcome to the Wiki

Welcome to TeaWiki, your mini online hub for all things tea! Whether you're a tea lover, a newbie, or just someone who enjoys a good cup of tea, you're in the right place.

TeaWiki is a simple site where you can find information about different types of tea. We cover everything from popular ones like green, black, and herbal teas to lesser-known varieties. Learn about where they come from, how they're made, and the health benefits they offer.

We also provide tips on how to make the perfect cup of tea. Discover the right brewing techniques, temperatures, and times for different teas. You'll become a tea expert in no time!
